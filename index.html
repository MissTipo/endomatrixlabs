The challenge

Hormonal health is often reduced to menstrual tracking, despite hormones shaping energy, mood, skin, metabolism, cognition, and wellbeing across a woman’s entire life.

Most women receive fragmented information, late diagnoses, or dismissal of symptoms that are deeply connected to endocrine change.

Our mission

To empower women with hormone-aware intelligence that supports informed, adaptive daily living across all life stages.

Our vision

A future where every woman has personalized endocrine insight that evolves with her body and supports better health outcomes over time.

EndoMatrix Core

EndoMatrix Core is a personalized, phase-aware intelligence layer that connects hormonal context to everyday decisions.

  • Hormonal phase modeling across cycles and life stages
  • Symptom-aware insight grounded in longitudinal data
  • Daily lifestyle guidance aligned with endocrine context
  • Continuous learning as the body changes over time

EndoMatrix Core is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace medical care.

The endocrine digital twin

Our long-term vision is a living endocrine digital twin — a continuously evolving model that reflects how an individual’s hormonal system changes across life.

This model is designed to support understanding, not diagnosis, and to act as a bridge between lived experience and clinical care.

Scientific & ethical foundation

  • Hormonal change is continuous, not episodic
  • Context and timing matter
  • Understanding precedes intervention
  • Clear boundaries between insight and diagnosis

Working alongside healthcare professionals

EndoMatrix Labs is built to complement clinical care, working alongside endocrinologists, dermatologists, nutritionists, physiologists, and women’s health specialists.

Our focus is longitudinal context and patient-reported insight, supporting better-informed conversations between individuals and clinicians.

Our roadmap

  • Phase 1: EndoMatrix Core
  • Phase 2: Endocrine Digital Twin
  • Phase 3: Ecosystem expansion and research partnerships
